Pizza Dip

Description

A gooey, cheesy pizza dip that is the perfect companion to any party you attend.

Ingredients:

  • 8 ounces softened cream cheese
  • 1.5 teaspoons Italian seasoning
  • .25 teaspoons garlic powder
  • 1 cup pizza sauce
  • 2 cups shreeded mozzarella cheese
  • .5 cup mini pepperoni
  • .25 cup sliced olives
  • .25 cup green bell peppers (finely diced)
  • 1 tablespoon chopped parsley

Instructions:

  1. Preheat the oven to 400 degrees F. Coat a 6 or 8 inch baking dish or skillet with cooking spray.
  2. Place the cream cheese in a bowl and add the Italian seasoning and garlic powder.
  3. Spread the cream cheese in an even layer on the bottom of the prepared dish.
  4. Spread the pizza sauce on top of the cream cheese.
  5. Add the mozzarella cheese and arrange the pepperoni, olives, and bell pepper to the top.
  6. Bake for 15 minutes or until cheese is melted.
  7. Sprinkle with parsley and serve.
